Multi-grade Sand & Activated Carbon Filters (MS & FRP Models)

Multi-grade Sand & Activated Carbon Filters (MS & FRP Models) offer highly effective filtration solutions for various water purification applications. These filters ensure the removal of suspended particles, chlorine, and organic compounds, providing clear and clean water.

Overview

Multi-grade Sand & Activated Carbon Filters are widely used in water treatment for their ability to remove suspended solids, organic matter, chlorine, and other impurities. These filters are available in two models: MS (Mild Steel) and FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic), offering a range of advantages for different applications.

Features

  • Efficient removal of suspended particles and chlorine from water.
  • Two models available: MS (Mild Steel) for industrial use and FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic) for corrosion-resistant, lightweight applications.
  • Longer filter life due to multi-layer sand filtration system.
  • Improves water quality by removing organic compounds, bacteria, and pollutants.
  • Low maintenance and cost-effective operation.

Process

The filtration process involves the following steps:

  1. Pre-filtration: Water passes through a coarse sand layer to remove large suspended particles.
  2. Multi-layer Sand Filtration: The water flows through multiple layers of sand of varying sizes, effectively removing smaller suspended solids.
  3. Activated Carbon Filtration: Water passes through an activated carbon layer that adsorbs chlorine, organic compounds, and other contaminants.
  4. Post-filtration: Water is now cleaner, with reduced chlorine and impurities, ready for further treatment or use.

Applications

  • Industrial Water Treatment: Effective for removing suspended solids and chlorine from industrial water sources.
  • Drinking Water Purification: Used in municipal and residential water systems for chlorine removal and improving water taste.
  • Wastewater Treatment: Helps in the removal of pollutants and organic compounds in wastewater treatment plants.
  • Aquarium Filtration: Provides clear and safe water by removing harmful substances from aquarium water.
