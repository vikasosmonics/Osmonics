M/s. Osmonics Corporation

M/s. OSMONICS CORPORATION, owned by P. Vinodan, with over 30 years of experience in water treatment, was founded in 2023 as an Authorized Dealer of M/s. ION EXCHANGE (INDIA) LTD. Serving Mumbai, Thane, Pune, Raigad, Tarapur, and nearby areas, we specialize in advanced water treatment solutions, including Packaged Sewage Recycling Systems for residential, industrial, and commercial applications. Our mission is to address water scarcity challenges by providing compact, cost-effective systems that enable the reuse of recycled water for non-potable purposes like gardening and flushing, promoting sustainability and environmental conservation.
