Process

The process of using spares, consumables, and chemicals in a water treatment system follows these key steps:

  1. Inspection: Regular inspection to identify parts that need replacement or chemicals that need replenishing.
  2. Ordering: Order the required spares, consumables, or chemicals based on the needs of the system.
  3. Replacement: Replace worn-out parts such as filters, membranes, or seals to ensure system efficiency.
  4. Application of Chemicals: Apply necessary chemicals for cleaning, disinfection, or pH adjustments.
  5. Maintenance: Ongoing maintenance using consumables to keep the system operating at optimal levels.
